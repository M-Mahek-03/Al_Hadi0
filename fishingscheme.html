Fisheries Welfare Schemes

Scheme Name Who is it for Eligibility Benefits Documents Apply
Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) Fishers, fish farmers, MSMEs, cooperatives, women, SC/ST beneficiaries Registered fishers/fish farmers with valid documents Subsidies (up to 60%), infrastructure, market linkages, safety kits, training Apply
Fisheries registration certificate, caste certificate (for SC/ST/Women), project proposal (if applicable), identity and address proof.
National Scheme For Welfare of Fishermen (NSWF) Small and marginal fishers families Registered small-scale fisherfolk Financial aid for housing, sanitation, education, community development Apply
Identity proof, fisher card registration, proof of residence.
Kisan Credit Card (KCC) for Fishers Registered traditional fishers and fish farmers Valid fisher registration/license Loans up to ₹5 lakh for fishing and allied activities at lower interest rates Apply
Proof of fishing activity, identity proof, address proof, fishing license/registration.
Fisheries Infrastructure Development Fund (FIDF) State agencies, cooperatives, entrepreneurs Fisheries-related entities with capital projects Loans and subsidies for harbor, cold storage, processing infrastructure Apply
Project proposal, approvals from fisheries department, financial documents of entity.
Marine Fishermen Assistance Program Registered marine fishermen Marine fisher registration Capital subsidies, savings schemes during lean seasons Contact local fisheries or maritime boards
Coastal fisher ID proof, registration certificates.
Skill Development & Training Programs (NFDB) Any fisher or youth linked to fisheries Registration with fisheries department or NFDB programs Training courses, certificates, exposure visits Apply
Registration documents for training, ID proof.
